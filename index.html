CS1 — Project Outline & Strategy

CHASE DYE
Fitness Modeling
Brand Launch
Digital Marketing

Project Focus

The Premise

About to graduate college with multiple digital marketing firms recruiting me, I chose to walk away and pursue the career I actually want — fitness modeling. This project documents the process of building that brand from zero: choosing the visual identity, developing the messaging, creating content, and establishing a presence across the platforms where fitness talent gets discovered and hired.

This is Scenario B — rebranding from scratch. The brand is me. The product is my presence in the fitness and athletic modeling space.

Project Overview

What This Project Builds

A complete content marketing strategy and personal brand built from the ground up — covering brand identity, audience development, platform strategy, visual content, video production, and written copy. Every deliverable is designed to position Chase Dye as a credible, bookable fitness model with a distinct look and a consistent online presence.

By the end of the semester, this portfolio will function like a real agency-level brand launch — one that can be shown to talent agencies, athletic brands, and sponsorship partners.

Goals & Measurement

Goal 1 — Awareness

Build brand recognition by publishing consistent, high-quality content across fitness-relevant platforms and establishing a professional online presence that attracts industry attention.

Goal 2 — Positioning

Establish Chase Dye as a credible fitness modeling brand through a strong visual identity, compelling storytelling, and content that showcases both physique and personality.

Measurement

Tracked through engagement metrics — views, impressions, likes, comments, shares, profile visits, and follower growth across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

Quality Check

Consistency of branding across all touchpoints, quality of visual and written assets, and completion of the full planned content schedule.

CS4 — Brand Story

BRANDING

Overall Story

What My Brand Is About

My brand started with fitness because that has been a big part of my life for years. As I got more interested in modeling and content creation, I realized I could bring all of those interests together instead of keeping them separate.

I want my content to show fitness, modeling, travel, clothing, and other parts of my everyday life. I do not want everything to look overly staged or like I am trying to be somebody else. Some content will be more planned out, but I also want to show the work and normal moments behind it.

The main idea is improving yourself and being confident in what you are working toward. Fitness helped me become more disciplined and comfortable with myself, and that is something I want people to see through my content.

I also want the brand to help me connect with fitness, clothing, lifestyle, and travel companies. My goal is to create content that still feels like me while also being something a brand would feel comfortable using.

Identity Brief

My Main Focus

My brand combines fitness modeling with lifestyle content. It exists because I want to build a career around things I already enjoy instead of making content that does not fit me. The focus is showing confidence, consistency, fitness, and personal growth in a way that feels real.

Personality

If My Brand Was a Person

01

Confident

Comfortable being seen without needing to act better than everyone.

02

Driven

Always working toward something instead of just talking about it.

03

Social

Friendly, outgoing, and able to connect with different people.

04

Active

Interested in working out, traveling, being outside, and trying things.

If the brand was an actual person, they would probably be wearing fitted gym clothes, swimwear, or simple streetwear. They would take their goals seriously but still know how to have fun. They would not like fake personalities or people who pretend to have everything figured out.

Brand Voice

How I Want It to Sound

I want the voice to sound confident and casual. It should feel like I am talking normally instead of reading from an advertisement. I can still motivate people, but I do not want every caption to sound like a speech.

The wording should usually be direct, positive, and easy to understand. A little humor is fine too because that is more natural for me.

Example

“Was not really feeling this workout at first, but we got it done.”

This sounds more like something I would actually post.

Brand Values

What Matters to Me

Confidence

Being comfortable with yourself and what you bring.

Consistency

Continuing to show up even when motivation is lower.

Growth

Trying to improve without pretending to be perfect.

Authenticity

Creating content that still feels connected to who I am.

CS6 — Content Draft

CREATE

Instagram Content Draft IDEAS

I decided to create 5 mock up Instagram posts because social media is where most people would first discover "my brand." These posts are meant to introduce who I am and build some level of trust with my audience. Also show the type of content I want to make with potential brands.

Type

Instagram Posts (5)

Software

Canva • Photoshop

Audience

Fitness, lifestyle and modeling audience

Goal

Build awareness and engagement

You Don't Need A Perfect Workout

Sometimes just showing up is enough. This post is meant to encourage an audience if people who feel like they have to be perfect before starting or even how they go about starting.

More Than The Gym

Fitness is a huge part of my life, but I also want people to see the lifestyle, and everyday side of my brand.

Confidence Takes Practice

This is an example more content that is for reflective or introspective moments. I would love my audience to find ways to reflect on their own life after seeing my content.

Behind the Scenes

Most people only see the finished photo or video, but there's usually a lot that happens before that. I wanted one post that showed the process because I think it makes the content feel more real.

Small Wins Add Up

I don't think progress always comes from huge changes. Most of it comes from doing the small things consistently. That's something I've learned from fitness and it's carried over into other parts of my life too.

How These Fit My Strategy

I didn't want every post to look the same. Some are motivational and some teach something. Together they give people a better idea of who I am and the type of content I want to keep creating. They also move someone from discovering my page towards getting to know me a little better.
