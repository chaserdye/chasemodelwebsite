CS1 — Project Outline & Strategy

CHASE DYE
Fitness Modeling
Brand Launch
Digital Marketing

Project Focus

The Premise

About to graduate college with multiple digital marketing firms recruiting me, I chose to walk away and pursue the career I actually want — fitness modeling. This project documents the process of building that brand from zero: choosing the visual identity, developing the messaging, creating content, and establishing a presence across the platforms where fitness talent gets discovered and hired.

This is Scenario B — rebranding from scratch. The brand is me. The product is my presence in the fitness and athletic modeling space.

Project Overview

What This Project Builds

A complete content marketing strategy and personal brand built from the ground up — covering brand identity, audience development, platform strategy, visual content, video production, and written copy. Every deliverable is designed to position Chase Dye as a credible, bookable fitness model with a distinct look and a consistent online presence.

By the end of the semester, this portfolio will function like a real agency-level brand launch — one that can be shown to talent agencies, athletic brands, and sponsorship partners.

Goals & Measurement

Goal 1 — Awareness

Build brand recognition by publishing consistent, high-quality content across fitness-relevant platforms and establishing a professional online presence that attracts industry attention.

Goal 2 — Positioning

Establish Chase Dye as a credible fitness modeling brand through a strong visual identity, compelling storytelling, and content that showcases both physique and personality.

Measurement

Tracked through engagement metrics — views, impressions, likes, comments, shares, profile visits, and follower growth across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

Quality Check

Consistency of branding across all touchpoints, quality of visual and written assets, and completion of the full planned content schedule.

CS2 — Buyer Personas & Target Audience

AUDIENCE

Target Audience

Who I'm Creating For

My content is created for people who value fitness, discipline, confidence, and an active lifestyle. They enjoy following athletes and creators who share authentic workouts, travel, fashion, and behind-the-scenes moments while inspiring others to become the best version of themselves.

My content is also designed for fitness brands, athletic apparel companies, supplement companies, gyms, photographers, and talent agencies looking for professional, high-quality visual content and authentic brand representation.
